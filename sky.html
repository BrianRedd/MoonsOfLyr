Best at Full Screen
  • Arrow forward/back to increment hour
  • Arrow up/down to increment day
  • Page up/down to increment year
  • Home to return to 1847:264:5
  • End to go to 1848:187:1 (convergence)
  • Esc to go to 0:0:5
  • NumPad * to move 45° longitudally
  • [/] to increment 500,526 hours (time between convergences)
  • q to move geographically to sun at zenith
  • w, e, and r to move geographically to where each moon is at zenith
  • Left/right click on numbers in info bar to increment up/down
